About Me

Hi! My name is Corey. I am a student of the coding bootcamp at Codecademy. I am currently learning full stack web development and so, I decided to make a project portfolio. I am hoping to gain more experience for a job in the tech industry and made this page to showcase my skills. Even though I have only started learning to code and web development, I am excited to learn more.

I, most recently, worked construction for the last 9 years. I mainly did carpentry work. I love to build things but don't want to destroy my body like so many others do in construction. My main true passion is computers and technology and thought i'll take my love to build and apply it to something in the tech industry. Thats when i decided to persue a career in full stack web development.

My other interests in tech are Cybersecurity and Software Development. I have always been fascinated with hacking and the different techniques. One of the reasons I started coding was to learn how to read it better and better my skills as an ethical hacker. Now, learning how to secure code has become fascinating to me also.

"Who Said That Quote?"

A browser based game where you guess which major public figure who said the quote. You test yourself to see how high you can score. Built with HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, and ran completely through the browser. Its still a work in progress. I have to finish more of the different categories and put together a highscore table. I built it for the browser with the idea I could expand on it and host it online. Eventually, implement an online live multiplayer mode possibly. Maybe even convert it to a mobile app to enjoy from the phone on the go.

If you would like to check out my progress so far you can find it on my github page.

"Jammmin"

A web app that allows users to create playlists of all there favorite songs. This is my first React WebApp. Utilizing the Spotify Api, your able to login to your Spotify account and create new or retrieve existing playlists. You can add or remove tracks as you like, Then save the changes back to your Spotify account. Plan on expanding the capabilities of this app like adding suggested songs based on the users top listened to music while there are no search results displayed. Maybe even some album art in the search results.
